Who We Are

An apolitical and non-profit organization conceived by like-minded volunteers

"Sevabrata" â€“ Dedicated to serving others. The name and the motto of the organisation are entwined. This apolitical and non-profit social organisation was conceived by a few like-minded volunteers, who believe in working in the spirit of service as proclaimed by Swami Vivekananda. Sevabrata aims to extend a helping hand to all those seeking medical guidance, as well as provide financial assistance, logistics, and psychological support related to it. Over time, the organisation expanded its scope to support needy students through the Sevabrata Academic Support (SAS) program. These ongoing efforts collectively inspired the formation of a formal organisation to serve all who seek support in their hours of need.

Since its inception, the Sevabrata Foundation has been dedicated to bridging the gap between the need for excellent healthcare services among low-income patients and the support they require. The foundation provides comprehensive guidance, financial assistance, and emotional support to underserved patients, while also helping students overcome challenges in continuing their education and pursuing their academic careers.

Lack of Knowledge

Medical Costs

Financial Insolvency

Families unable to afford necessary medical treatments or education fees

Rural Healthcare Gap

Unavailability of critical care facilities in rural areas

Our Mission

Comprehensive support for medical care accessibility

While we understand that we cannot help every hand in need, within our capabilities, we have pledged to reach out to people as much as possible. This selfless endeavour to help people, particularly in need of medical assistance & academic support, is driven by the urge to give back to society in our simple ways. At Sevabrata, we are a group of people from diverse backgrounds and all over the world, united by a single purpose: to help bridge the affordability gap in healthcare and education for those experiencing poverty every step of the way.
