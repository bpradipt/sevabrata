Our Approach:

As above mentioned, Sevabrata would give an end to end financial assistance to the patient. As an organization, the funds in our reserve would be used. And thereafter, through various campaigns we would raise money as per the requirement.

Listed below are our various activities pertaining to the requirements of our patient:

  • Raising funds for the treatment of the patients through global campaign
  • Finding appropriate doctors and hospitals for best and economic treatment
  • Providing guidance and logistics support to patient during the treatment
  • Supporting post treatment care up to rehabilitation on case to case basis
  • Providing support covering mental, moral and emotional sides of the patient and his/her family

Taking up activities in other fields of services may happen based on the situation and agreement of members.
